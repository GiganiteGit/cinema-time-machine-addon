Cinema Time Machine

Pick a year. See what audiences were watching.

A Stremio catalog addon that lets you browse film and TV by year — choose any year from the dropdown in Discover and the rows fill with what was on then, plus a “This Week, Years Ago” row of films released this week in years past.

Install in Stremio

This product uses TMDB and the TMDB APIs but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.
